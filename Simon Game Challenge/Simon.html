How to play

  • The game starts by highlighting one of the colored buttons with a light and sound.
  • Memorize the sequence of lights as the game progresses.
  • Click the buttons in the exact order they were highlighted.
  • After each correct round, a new color is added to the sequence.
  • If you press a button out of order or choose the wrong color, the game ends.
  • Try to complete the longest sequence possible to achieve a high score.
